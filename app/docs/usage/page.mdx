# Usage Guide

Learn how to use Solidarity for secure, privacy-focused business card sharing in various scenarios.

## Overview

Solidarity puts you in control of your networking privacy with:

- **Three privacy levels** (Public, Professional, Personal)
- **Multiple sharing methods** (QR codes, P2P, Apple Wallet)
- **Zero-knowledge proofs** for anonymous verification
- **Local-first approach** - your data never leaves your device

Choose the section that best fits your needs, or start with the Quick Start guide to create your first card.
