# Usage Guide

Learn how to use Solidarity for secure, privacy-focused business card sharing in various scenarios.

## Quick Start

### Creating Your Digital Business Card

1. **Open Solidarity** on your iPhone
2. **Tap "Create Card"** to start building your profile
3. **Fill in your information:**
   - Name (required)
   - Job title and company
   - Contact details (email, phone)
   - Social media links
   - Profile photo
4. **Choose privacy levels** for different types of sharing

### Privacy Levels Explained

**Public** - Information visible to everyone
- Basic contact info
- Professional title
- Company name

**Professional** - Shared with verified business contacts
- Full contact details
- Direct phone number
- Professional social media

**Personal** - Only for trusted connections
- Personal phone number
- Personal social accounts
- Private email addresses

## Common Use Cases

### 1. Networking Events

**Scenario:** You're at a conference and want to quickly exchange contact information.

**How to use:**
1. Open Solidarity and select your card
2. Choose "Professional" privacy level
3. Generate QR code or use AirDrop-style sharing
4. Let others scan your code or accept their connection

**Benefits:**
- No need to carry physical cards
- Instant, contactless sharing
- Control what information you share
- Automatic contact organization

### 2. Business Meetings

**Scenario:** Meeting new clients or partners in a professional setting.

**How to use:**
1. Create a "Professional" version of your card
2. Use peer-to-peer sharing for direct exchange
3. Verify group membership if applicable (e.g., same company)
4. Add to Apple Wallet for easy access

**Benefits:**
- Professional appearance
- Verified identity through zero-knowledge proofs
- Seamless integration with existing workflows

### 3. Casual Social Connections

**Scenario:** Meeting someone at a social event or through mutual friends.

**How to use:**
1. Switch to "Personal" privacy level
2. Share only the information you're comfortable with
3. Use QR code for quick sharing
4. Update privacy settings later if needed

**Benefits:**
- Control over personal information
- Easy to update or revoke access
- No spam or unwanted contact

### 4. Group Verification

**Scenario:** Proving membership in an organization without revealing identity.

**How to use:**
1. Join verified groups (company, organization, etc.)
2. Generate zero-knowledge proof of membership
3. Share proof without revealing personal details
4. Others can verify your membership anonymously

**Benefits:**
- Privacy-preserving verification
- No central authority needed
- Cryptographically secure

## Sharing Methods

### QR Code Sharing

**Best for:** Large groups, presentations, public sharing

1. Select your card and privacy level
2. Tap "Generate QR Code"
3. Display on your phone screen
4. Others scan with their camera or Solidarity app

### Peer-to-Peer Sharing

**Best for:** Direct, secure exchanges

1. Both users open Solidarity
2. Enable "Nearby Sharing"
3. Select the person from discovered devices
4. Choose privacy level and send

### Apple Wallet Integration

**Best for:** Quick access and professional appearance

1. Create a Wallet pass from your card
2. Add to Apple Wallet
3. Access from lock screen or Wallet app
4. Show QR code for scanning

## Privacy & Security

### Your Data Stays Local

- All cards stored encrypted on your device
- No cloud storage or central servers
- You control who gets what information

### Zero-Knowledge Proofs

- Prove group membership without revealing identity
- Cryptographically secure verification
- No trusted third parties required

### Granular Privacy Controls

- Different information for different contexts
- Update privacy levels anytime
- Revoke access when needed

## Tips for Best Experience

### Organizing Your Cards

- Create different versions for different contexts
- Use descriptive names (e.g., "Conference Card", "Client Meeting")
- Regularly update information

### Managing Connections

- Review received cards regularly
- Organize by event or context
- Follow up with important connections

### Privacy Best Practices

- Start with minimal information sharing
- Increase privacy level as trust builds
- Regularly audit your shared information
- Use group verification for sensitive contexts

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Issues

**Problem:** Can't connect to nearby devices
**Solution:** 
- Check Bluetooth and WiFi are enabled
- Ensure both devices have Solidarity open
- Try moving closer together

### QR Code Not Scanning

**Problem:** QR code won't scan properly
**Solution:**
- Increase screen brightness
- Hold steady for 2-3 seconds
- Try different angles or distances

### Missing Information

**Problem:** Received card is missing expected information
**Solution:**
- Check the privacy level used for sharing
- Ask sender to reshare with appropriate level
- Verify you're in the right group if applicable
