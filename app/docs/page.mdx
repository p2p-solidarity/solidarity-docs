# Solidarity

**Privacy-first business card sharing** that puts you in control. Share your contact information securely without compromising your privacy or relying on cloud services.

## What is Solidarity?

Solidarity is a mobile app that lets you create, share, and manage digital business cards with advanced privacy controls. Unlike traditional networking apps, everything works offline and your data never leaves your device.

## How It Works

1. **Create your digital business card** with different privacy levels
2. **Choose what to share** based on the situation (public/professional/personal)
3. **Share instantly** via QR code, device-to-device, or Apple Wallet
4. **Verify group membership** using zero-knowledge proofs when needed
